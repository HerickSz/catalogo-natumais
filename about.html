BACKGROUND: Clout Store is an online boutique mainly operating in Lesotho. It was established back in 2019, on the 16th of June.It first started operating via whatsapp business, where products were advertised as whatsapp statuses. Later on established an instagram page and a facebook page which became very pivotal to the rapid growth of the store.The store started out selling clothes only, but now it also .

MANAGEMENT: The Clout Store is under the management and supervision of a board of five members. Chairman of the board and major shareholder being Mr Motlalepula Pepenene, who is also the sole founder of the store(picture displayed on the right).He is a young aspiring businessman,a self taught web developer and business manager.The store is structured into three sectors, Administration sector, financial department and deliveries department.

HOW WE OPERATE: As an online boutique, we dont have a physical store. We have delivery spots in every urban district across the country(In Maseru we deliver at sefika complex, In Leribe we deliver at Maputsoe.In Mafeteng we deliver at hospital area.In Berea we deliver at TY Hall).

OUR VISION: The Clout Store aspires to be one the world's top online clothing stores if not the best.
